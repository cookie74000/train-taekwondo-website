Your Instructors

Two experienced martial arts instructors — one from the UK, one based in Mallorca — combining their expertise for a truly unique programme.

GC

Gavin Cook

5th Dan · TAGB · Train Taekwondo Schools

Gavin is the founder of Train Taekwondo Schools, operating five locations across East Yorkshire. With over 43 years of Taekwondo experience and a 5th Dan black belt through the Taekwondo Association of Great Britain, Gavin brings world-class technical instruction in ITF patterns, kicking, sparring, and breaking. He has trained and graded hundreds of students from white belt through to black belt.

ITF Patterns Kicking Technique Sparring Breaking Grading
IT

Ioannis Tziolas

ITF Instructor · Son Serra de Marina, Mallorca

Ioannis is a Mallorca-based ITF Taekwondo instructor with a passion for blending martial arts with ancient Greek philosophical tradition. His unique Peripatetic teaching system — combining nature walks, guided self-discovery, and reflective discussion — creates a learning experience unlike any conventional training camp. He owns and operates the retreat property and dojang in Son Serra de Marina.

Self-Defence Greek Philosophy Peripatetic Teaching Yoga Mindfulness

The Venue

A private property in the quiet coastal village of Son Serra de Marina — with a purpose-built dojang attached, accommodation for the whole group, and Sa Canova Beach a short walk away.

🏠

Private Retreat House

5 double bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, 3 kitchens. The whole property is exclusively yours for the week — no other guests. Both instructors stay on site throughout.

🥋

Purpose-Built Dojang

Two fully matted training rooms attached to the property. Red foam puzzle mats, punch bag, boxing gloves, TRX straps, yoga mats, and outdoor courtyard — train morning and evening without leaving the site.

🏖️

Sa Canova Beach

A beautiful, unspoiled beach a short walk from the property — used for morning yoga and outdoor training sessions in the fresh Mediterranean air. The GR222 coastal trail runs nearby for Peripatetic walks.

Who Is This For?

This retreat is designed for anyone who wants to deepen their Taekwondo practice in a focused, supportive, and beautiful environment.

Existing Train Taekwondo Students

Students of any grade from Train Taekwondo Schools looking to fast-track their development, refine their patterns, and experience training in a dedicated environment.

Taekwondo Practitioners from Other Clubs

ITF or WTF practitioners from any club who want to experience a different approach to training — combining technical instruction with philosophy and wellness.

Martial Arts Beginners

No prior experience required. The programme is structured to welcome complete beginners alongside experienced practitioners. All sessions are adapted to your level.

Yoga & Wellness Enthusiasts

If you're drawn to the yoga, philosophy, and wellness elements and want to try martial arts in a safe, supportive setting, this retreat is the perfect introduction.

Getting There

Son Serra de Marina, Mallorca

A quiet, unspoiled coastal village on the north-east coast of Mallorca — far from the tourist crowds, surrounded by nature, and just a short walk from one of the island's most beautiful beaches.

✈️

Fly to Palma Airport (PMI) — direct flights from most UK airports. Ryanair, easyJet, and Jet2 all operate routes from Leeds Bradford, Manchester, and London.

🚗

Transfer to Son Serra de Marina — approximately 1 hour from Palma Airport by car. Car hire is recommended; taxis and transfers are also available.

🏖️

Sa Canova Beach — a 5-minute walk from the retreat house. Used for morning yoga and outdoor training sessions throughout the week.

🥋

The Dojang — purpose-built and attached directly to the retreat house. No travel required between accommodation and training.
