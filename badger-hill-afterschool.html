About the Club

What Your Child Will Experience

Skillz — Martial Arts for Every Child

Skillz is a dynamic, Taekwondo-based martial arts programme designed for children of all abilities. Its high-energy, fast-paced style makes it one of the most engaging after-school activities available — combining fitness, coordination, and mental focus in every session.

Each 45-minute class is structured to challenge and reward. Children progress through a clear syllabus, building physical skills alongside confidence, self-discipline, and respect — values that carry far beyond the Dojang.

After completing the after-school club, children and their parents are invited to attend 2 free training sessions at one of our local Train Taekwondo venues to continue their journey.

What Children Will Develop

  • ✓ Physical fitness, flexibility, and coordination
  • ✓ Self-confidence and a positive self-image
  • ✓ Focus, listening skills, and concentration
  • ✓ Respect for themselves and others
  • ✓ Self-discipline and emotional control
  • ✓ Teamwork and social skills
  • ✓ A love of movement and active lifestyle

TAGB Affiliated · UK Sport Recognised
Train Taekwondo Schools has been creating champions for life since 2011. All instructors are fully DBS checked, first-aid trained, and TAGB certified.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need any experience?
Not at all. The club is open to complete beginners and all KS1 & KS2 pupils regardless of experience. Sessions are structured so children of all levels can participate and progress together.
What does my child need to wear?
Comfortable sports clothing and bare feet or clean indoor trainers. No specialist equipment is required for the after-school club sessions.
Where do I collect my child?
Please collect your child from the main reception at 4:00pm promptly. Sessions finish at 4:00pm and children will not be left unattended.
What happens if a session is cancelled?
In the unlikely event of a cancellation, you will be notified as early as possible and the session will be rescheduled or a refund arranged for that week.
Can my child continue after the club ends?
Yes! Children who complete the after-school club are invited to attend 2 free training sessions at one of our local Train Taekwondo venues. It's a great way to continue the journey and try a full class.
Is the instructor DBS checked?
Yes. Mr Gavin Cook is fully DBS checked, TAGB certified, and first-aid trained. Train Taekwondo Schools has been operating safely in schools across East Yorkshire since 2011.
