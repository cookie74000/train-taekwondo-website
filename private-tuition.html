Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need for the session?

Just a device with a camera (phone, tablet or laptop), a Google account to join Google Meet, and enough space to move around safely. Wear your dobok if you have one.

How do I pay?

Payment details will be provided when Gavin confirms your session. Payment is due before the session takes place.

When will I receive my progress report?

Your PDF progress report will be emailed to you within 24 hours of your session completing.

Can I book multiple sessions?

Absolutely — many students book regular sessions in the run-up to grading or competition. Each session includes a fresh progress report tracking your improvement.

Is this suitable for beginners?

Yes — private tuition is available for all grades from white belt upwards, including Ninja Skillz students. Sessions are tailored to your current level and goals.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Please give at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Just reply to Gavin's confirmation email and he'll arrange an alternative time.
