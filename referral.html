How the Referral Programme Works

Three simple steps — and your next month of training could be completely free.

1

Invite a Friend

Send them a free taster session via the app, or simply tell them about the club. Anyone new is welcome — friends, family, classmates, or coworkers.

2

They Mention Your Name

When your friend registers, they mention your full name. That's all it takes — no codes, no forms, no hassle.

3

You Earn a Free Month

Once your friend completes enrolment and payment, your free month is credited to your account automatically. Earn one for every new student you refer.

Who Can You Refer?

✅ Eligible Referrals
  • 👫Friends and family members
  • 🏫Classmates or school friends
  • 💼Coworkers or neighbours
  • 🥋Anyone joining Accelerated or Basic Tuition
  • 🥷New Ninja Skillz or Spectrum Skillz students
❌ Not Eligible
  • 🔄Students currently enrolled at the club
  • ⏮️Previously enrolled students returning
  • 🎟️Trial or short-term programme participants (at school discretion)

Ready to Earn Your Free Month?

The easiest way to refer a friend is to send them a free taster session directly from the app. They get to try a class for free — and if they join, you train for free too.

Why Refer a Friend?

💰

Save on Your Training

Every referral earns you a full month free. Refer enough friends and your training costs nothing.

💪

Help Friends Get Fit

Taekwondo builds confidence, discipline, and fitness. Share something that genuinely changes lives.

🥋

Strengthen Our Dojang

Training with people you know makes every class better. Help grow our Taekwondo family.

🏆

No Limit on Rewards

There's no cap. Every new student you bring in earns you another free month — indefinitely.

Programme Terms

📅 Free months are applied to future membership dues only.
✅ Referral credit is issued after the new student completes enrolment and payment.
🔒 Free months are non-transferable and have no cash value.
🥋 Referral rewards apply only to active members in good standing.
📈 Multiple referrals may be accumulated and applied consecutively.
⚠️ Trial students or short-term programmes may not qualify (at school discretion).
📋 Train Taekwondo Schools reserves the right to modify or discontinue this programme at any time.
