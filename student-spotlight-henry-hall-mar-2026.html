Meet Henry

Meet Henry Hall — nearly 10 years old, 6.5 years into his Taekwondo journey, and one of the most inspiring students in our dojang. Henry is visually impaired and works harder than anyone to earn every step of his progress. He embodies the tenet of Indomitable Spirit every single session — turning up, working hard, and never making excuses. Henry is proof that Taekwondo is for everyone.

"I just love Taekwondo. It makes me feel strong and I've made loads of friends here."

— Henry Hall, March 2026
In His Own Words
What's your favourite kick?
Side kick — it feels really powerful when I get it right.
What's your favourite pattern?
Chon-Ji — it was the first one I learned and I still love it.
What's been your biggest challenge in Taekwondo?
Sometimes it's hard to see what Mr Cook is showing us, but I just ask and everyone helps me.
What's your proudest Taekwondo moment?
Getting my green stripe — I trained really hard for it and it felt amazing.
Which tenet of Taekwondo means the most to you?
Indomitable Spirit. Mr Cook says it means never giving up and I think that's really important.
What would you say to someone thinking about starting Taekwondo?
Do it! It's really fun and everyone is really nice. You'll get better really quickly.
What do you enjoy most about coming to class?
Seeing my friends and learning new things. And the sparring — I love sparring!
Quick-Fire Fun Facts

Something loud and fast!

Chicken nuggets — every time

Gaming and watching football

Spider-Man — he never gives up either!

All my friends in the dojang
Next Goal
Green belt — I'm going to work really hard for it
A Note from Mr Cook

Henry is one of those students who makes you remember exactly why you became an instructor. He faces challenges every single session that most of us will never experience, and yet he shows up every week with a smile on his face, a willingness to work, and a determination that puts many adults to shame. He never complains, never makes excuses, and never gives up.

Henry is a living example of Indomitable Spirit — the tenet that says no matter what stands in your way, you keep going. He inspires every student and instructor in our dojang, and we are incredibly proud to name him our March 2026 Student Spotlight. Henry, you are an absolute star. Keep going — we can't wait to see what you achieve next.
