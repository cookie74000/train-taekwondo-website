Why It Works for Everyone

A Genuine Win-Win

This isn't a sales pitch dressed up as a school visit. It's a real, high-energy, curriculum-linked Taekwondo experience that benefits everyone involved — completely free of charge.

🏫 For Your School

  • A free, high-quality PE session delivered by a qualified instructor
  • Supports PSHE, wellbeing and physical literacy goals
  • Builds confidence, focus and self-discipline in pupils
  • Anti-bullying and resilience themes woven throughout
  • No preparation required — Gavin brings everything
  • Suitable for Reception through to Year 6
  • Full or half-day options to fit your timetable
  • Zero cost to the school — completely free

🥋 For the Club

  • A chance to introduce Taekwondo to new young people
  • Pupils who love it can join our after-school or weekend classes
  • Parents receive a free taster pass for their child
  • Builds community presence and trust in the local area
  • Gavin gets to do what he loves — inspire young people

Honest transparency: Gavin will let pupils know about his classes at the end of the session — but there's no hard sell, no pressure, and no obligation. The day is genuinely about the pupils first.

Simple Process

How It Works

1

Get in Touch

Drop Gavin a message via WhatsApp, email or the form below. Tell him your school, year groups, and preferred dates.

2

We Plan Together

Gavin will confirm availability, discuss the format (full or half day), and tailor the session to your year groups.

3

Gavin Comes In

He arrives with everything needed — pads, equipment, and a structured session plan. Your staff can watch or join in!

4

Pupils Get a Free Pass

Every child goes home with a free taster pass for a local class. No obligation — just an invitation to try it again.

On the Day

What Your Pupils Can Expect

🥋

Basic Techniques

Stances, blocks, punches and kicks — taught step by step in a fun, safe and age-appropriate way.

🧠

Focus & Discipline

Short drills that build concentration and self-control — the same skills that transfer directly to the classroom.

💪

Confidence Building

Every child is celebrated. Gavin's teaching style ensures every pupil — regardless of ability — feels successful.

🛡️

Anti-Bullying Messages

Age-appropriate conversations about standing tall, speaking up, and using confidence — not aggression.

🎮

Games & Activities

Taekwondo-based games that get every child moving, laughing and engaged — even the reluctant ones.

🏅

A Moment to Remember

Gavin ends every session with a mini "graduation" — every pupil earns a certificate or sticker to take home.

Example Schedule

Sample Day Timetable

Every taster day is tailored to your school's timetable and year groups. Below is a typical full-day structure — half-day options are equally available and just as impactful.

Note: This is an example only. Gavin will work with your school to create a schedule that fits your existing timetable, hall availability and year group structure. Sessions can be run back-to-back for multiple classes throughout the day.
Time Session Year Group Duration Focus
9:00 – 9:45 Morning Session 1
Warm-up & Basic Techniques		 Reception / Year 1 45 mins Fun movement, stances, listening skills
9:45 – 10:30 Morning Session 2
Kicks, Blocks & Games		 Year 2 / Year 3 45 mins Coordination, focus, teamwork
10:30 – 10:45 Break — 15 mins —
10:45 – 11:30 Mid-Morning Session
Patterns & Partner Work		 Year 3 / Year 4 45 mins Memory, discipline, respect
11:30 – 12:15 Pre-Lunch Session
Anti-Bullying & Confidence		 Year 4 / Year 5 45 mins Resilience, self-belief, speaking up
12:15 – 1:00 Lunch Break — 45 mins —
1:00 – 1:45 Afternoon Session 1
Sparring Drills & Pad Work		 Year 5 / Year 6 45 mins Controlled contact, confidence, fun
1:45 – 2:30 Afternoon Session 2
Mini Tournament & Graduation		 All Year Groups 45 mins Celebration, certificates, free taster passes
2:30 – 3:00 Optional: Staff Session Staff / Teachers 30 mins Fun intro for staff — always popular!

Half-Day Option: Choose either the morning (9:00–12:15) or afternoon (1:00–3:00) block. Gavin can cover 3–4 class groups in a half day. Perfect if your hall is only available for part of the day.

Your Instructor

About Gavin Cook

Gavin Cook is a 5th Dan Black Belt and Chief Instructor at Train Taekwondo Schools. He has been teaching Taekwondo since 2011 and has produced over 100 black belts across East Yorkshire.

Gavin is a specialist in teaching children and young people, with particular expertise in early years development, SEND-inclusive teaching, and using martial arts as a tool for building life skills. He is DBS checked, TAGB qualified, and fully insured.

He has delivered school taster days across East Yorkshire and is passionate about giving every child the chance to experience Taekwondo — regardless of background or ability.
