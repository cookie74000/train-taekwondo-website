What is Spectrum Skillz?

Helping Your Child Learn & Grow at Their Pace

Spectrum Skillz™ is an internationally-acclaimed, specialist programme that introduces specialised skill-based training for children who are on the autism spectrum, or who experience physical, intellectual, social or emotional limitations.

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is characterised by impaired communication and social interaction. Children with ASD tend to have average or above-average intelligence — but the communication difficulties that come with ASD can directly impact learning, because language skills are often underdeveloped.

The Spectrum Skillz programme uses game-based learning as a tool to develop missing skills — helping with language development, concentration, focus and social confidence in a safe, structured and fun environment.

The best thing about this programme is that it helps develop the child as a whole — a place where many other programmes fall short.

Does Your Child Have Trouble With…?

Spectrum Skillz was created to address the four key areas where children on the spectrum often need the most support.

🏃

Physical Limitations

Your child shows developmental delays. They may not always respond when called. Sometimes they flap their hands, rock their body or spin in circles — and structured movement can feel overwhelming.

🧠

Intellectual Limitations

Your child gives unrelated answers to questions, or repeats words and phrases over and over. They use few gestures and find it difficult to follow multi-step instructions or focus on new topics.

👥

Social Limitations

No matter what you do, it can be hard to comfort your child when they're stressed. They avoid eye contact, prefer to play alone and find group activities confusing or overwhelming.

💛

Emotional Limitations

Your child gets upset by minor changes but struggles to talk about their feelings. They may have meltdowns or become aggressive at times — and traditional classes can make this worse, not better.

If any of these sound familiar, Spectrum Skillz was designed with your child in mind. Our small, specialist classes provide the structure, patience and expertise your child needs to flourish.

Built Around How the Spectrum Brain Works

Spectrum Skillz is not a traditional martial arts class with autism adaptations bolted on. It is a programme built from the ground up around the specific cognitive and developmental profile of children on the spectrum.

🧠

Sensory Processing

Children with ASD may overreact or underreact to sensory stimuli. Our classes are designed to be low-stimulation and predictable — reducing sensory overwhelm so children can focus on learning.

🔄

Executive Functioning

Children with ASD often struggle with organising, planning and problem-solving. Every class follows a consistent, predictable structure that supports executive functioning development over time.

🎮

Game-Based Learning

SKILLZ uses game-based learning as its primary teaching tool — because play is the natural language of children. Games create motivating, low-pressure opportunities to develop language, attention and social skills.

💬

Language Development

Since ASD affects communication, it affects cognitive development. Every Spectrum Skillz activity is designed to create natural, motivating opportunities to develop language skills and attention simultaneously.

👥

Social Skill Building

The small class size (maximum 7) means every child gets individual attention and the chance to practise peer interaction in a safe, structured and supported environment — without the overwhelm of a large group.

🏆

Whole-Child Development

Spectrum Skillz develops children across all four domains simultaneously — physical, intellectual, emotional and social. This is what sets it apart from programmes that focus on only one area at a time.
