FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use this?
None at all. I handle the entire setup. You'll need a Facebook Business account (free) and a WhatsApp number — I guide you through the 5-minute Meta setup and do everything else myself.
Will it work on my existing WhatsApp number?
Yes. The agent runs on the WhatsApp Business API which can be connected to your existing number. Your number stays the same — it just gains AI capabilities.
What happens if someone asks something the bot doesn't know?
The AI is trained on your school's specific information. If someone asks something outside its knowledge, it politely tells them it doesn't have that information and directs them to contact you directly.
Can I update the class times or prices myself?
Yes — the knowledge base is a simple file I can update for you, or I can show you how to edit it yourself. Updates typically take under 10 minutes and redeploy automatically.
Does it work for all martial arts styles?
Absolutely. The system works for Taekwondo, Karate, BJJ, MMA, Kickboxing, Judo, or any other style. The knowledge base is fully customised to your school.
How long does setup take?
Typically 24–48 hours from when you send me your school details. The Professional package includes a setup call to go through everything together.
What booking platforms does it integrate with?
Any platform with a URL — ClubManager, TeamUp, Mindbody, PayPal, Stripe, or even just a Google Form. After registration, the bot sends a button linking to your chosen platform.
