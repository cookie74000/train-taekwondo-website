Thank you so much for registering with Train Taekwondo Schools. Whether you are signing up for yourself, your child, or the whole family, you have just taken the first step on an incredible journey — one that goes far beyond kicks and belts.

Meet Gavin Cook

Gavin Cook
5th Dan Black Belt  ·  Chief Instructor  ·  TAGB Certified

Gavin has been training in Taekwondo for over 43 years and has been running Train Taekwondo Schools for 15 years. As a 5th Dan Black Belt and fully qualified TAGB instructor, Gavin has dedicated his career to helping children, juniors and families discover the life-changing benefits of martial arts.

From his early days as a competitive fighter and TAGB champion, to building one of East Yorkshire's most respected Taekwondo schools — every technique Gavin teaches, he has competed with at the highest level. Gavin is also a certified Ninja Skillz instructor, trained in the brain-based child development programme that has transformed how we teach children aged 3–7. His passion is simple: to help every child and adult who walks through the door become the best version of themselves.

Your Journey

From White Belt to Black Belt — The 5-Year Journey

One of the most common questions we get asked is: "How long does it take to get a black belt?" With the TAGB, the honest answer for a dedicated student is around five years — and every step of that journey is structured, celebrated and deeply rewarding.

We grade three times a year — in February/March, June/July and October/November — giving every student a regular opportunity to demonstrate their progress. Since opening in 2011, we have proudly produced over 100 black belts.

  • White Belt — Starting OutYour first steps on the mat. Learning the basics of stance, movement and the TAGB ethos.
  • Yellow Tag & Yellow BeltBuilding confidence and coordination. First grading opportunity — a huge milestone.
  • Green Tag & Green BeltTechniques become more fluid. Patterns (Tuls) and sparring are introduced.
  • Blue Tag & Blue BeltStrength and precision develop. Students begin competing at regional level.
  • Red Tag & Red BeltAdvanced techniques and patterns. The final stage before the black belt journey begins.
  • Black Belt — 1st Dan & BeyondThe beginning of mastery — not the end. Typically achieved in around 5 years.
Grading Window Typical Months Notes
🌱 Spring Grading February / March First grading of the year
☀️ Summer Grading June / July Mid-year progress check
🍂 Autumn Grading October / November End-of-year milestone

Grading is always optional — there is never any pressure to grade before you are ready. Your instructor will let you know when you are ready to step up.

First Session

What to Bring to Your First Session

✅  Comfortable clothing — tracksuit bottoms and a t-shirt are perfect. No need for a dobok (uniform) yet.

✅  Bare feet — we train without shoes on the mat.

✅  Water bottle — sessions are energetic!

✅  An open mind and a smile — that's all we ask.

You will be able to purchase your dobok and TAGB licence after your taster session if you decide to join. Your instructor will guide you through everything.
