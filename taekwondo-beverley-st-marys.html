Who Is This Class For?

Taekwondo is for everyone — whatever your age, fitness level or experience.

🧒

Children (Ages 7+)

A brilliant activity that builds discipline, confidence and fitness. Children thrive in our structured, encouraging environment.

👨‍👩‍👧

Families

Train together as a family. Parents and children on the mat side by side — it's one of the most rewarding things you can do together.

🧑

Adults & Teens

Whether you're looking for fitness, self-defence, or a new challenge — Taekwondo delivers all three in one class.

🔰

Complete Beginners

No experience needed. Everyone starts at white belt. Our instructors make sure you feel welcome and confident from day one.

🏅 Official Affiliation

TAGB-Affiliated & Fully Insured

Train Taekwondo Schools is fully affiliated with the Taekwondo Association of Great Britain (TAGB) — one of the UK's largest and most respected Taekwondo organisations. Your child trains on an official syllabus with nationally recognised belt gradings.
