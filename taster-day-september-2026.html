Stamford Bridge Primary School Taster Day

We're visiting Stamford Bridge Primary School on Thursday 26th June with a fun taster session for Reception, KS1 and KS2 children. It's a brilliant opportunity to try Taekwondo before the new term in September — no kit, no experience needed, just come and give it a go!

What We Offer

The Right Class for Every Child

We run age-specific classes so every child trains at the right level and pace. From our youngest Ninja Skillz students aged 3–4, through to our Junior and Family Taekwondo classes for KS2 and beyond — there is a place for everyone.

We are also proud to be launching Spectrum Skillz in September — a dedicated class designed for children with additional needs, delivered with specialist knowledge and a supportive, structured environment.

Full details of all our classes, curriculum and what to expect are available on our website: www.traintaekwondo.co.uk
