Why You Should Enter

🆕 Perfect for First-Timers

Never competed before? The Lincs Champs is the ideal first competition. The atmosphere is brilliant, the organisation is excellent, and even brand new White Belt students can enter and medal.

🏆 We Have a Strong Track Record

Train Taekwondo Schools students have been on the podium at the Lincolnshire Championships year after year — in patterns, sparring, flying kicks and destruction. You could be next.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 We Go as a Squad

We always take a big group of students, so you'll never be there alone. There's a real team spirit on competition day — everyone cheers for each other.

📈 It Accelerates Your Progress

Students who compete consistently progress faster. The focus, discipline and experience of competing — win or lose — builds character and improves your Taekwondo more than almost anything else.

🎯 Multiple Events Available

You can enter patterns, sparring, flying kicks and more — giving you multiple chances to medal in a single day. Most students enter at least two events.

🤝 Supported by Our Black Belt Team

Our experienced team of black belts will be there on the day to warm you up, corner you in sparring, and support the whole squad. You won't be left to figure it out alone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I enter if I've never competed before?

Absolutely — this is one of the best competitions for first-timers. The Lincs Champs is well-organised, friendly, and welcomes students of all grades including brand new White Belts.

What belt do I need to be to enter?

Any belt — including White Belt. There are categories for every grade, age group and weight category. Even Tiny Tots can enter.

How do I enter?

Pay your entry fee through our online store using the link on this page. Once payment is confirmed, you will be added to the Train Taekwondo Schools master entry form and submitted on your behalf before the closing date.

What events can I enter?

Patterns, Point Sparring, Flying Kicks (Juniors up to 15yrs), Speed Kicking (Juniors up to 15yrs). Black Belts over 16 can also enter Continuous Sparring and Destruction. You can enter multiple events.

What do I need to bring on the day?

Full Taekwondo dobok (uniform), your TAGB licence, sparring equipment (hogu, head guard, gloves, shin/instep guards, gum shield), and plenty of water and snacks. The full kit list will be confirmed closer to the date via the competition WhatsApp group.

Will the team be there?

Yes — our experienced team of black belts will be there throughout the day to warm you up, corner you in sparring, and support the whole squad. You won't be alone.

What's the closing date?

Friday 29th May 2026. Entries cannot be accepted after this date as the master form must be submitted to the organisers. Please enter early.
