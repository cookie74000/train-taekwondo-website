💳 Payment

How Payment Works

When you click "Join Now" you'll be taken to our secure Club Manager registration form. You'll enter your details and set up your monthly payment by card. Your first payment is taken immediately, and your membership then renews automatically on the same date each month.

You can cancel at any time with one month's notice — just let Gavin know by WhatsApp or email and he'll sort it for you.

🔒
Secure Payments via Club Manager
All payments are processed securely through Club Manager — the same system used by thousands of martial arts clubs across the UK. Your card details are never stored by us.
