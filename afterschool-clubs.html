Good to Know

Everything You Need to Know

👕

No Uniform Required

Children can attend in their school PE kit or comfortable sports clothing. No special equipment is needed to get started.

🥋

All Abilities Welcome

Complete beginners are always welcome. Our sessions are structured so that children of all experience levels can participate and progress together.

🔒

DBS Checked Instructors

All Train Taekwondo instructors are fully DBS checked, TAGB certified, and first-aid trained. Your child's safety is our top priority.

📋

Term-Based Booking

Clubs run on a term-by-term basis. Payment secures your child's place for the full term. Places are limited so early booking is recommended.
