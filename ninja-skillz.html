Built Around Your Child's Brain

The Ninja Skillz programme is specifically designed for children aged 3–7 who are not yet ready for mainstream Taekwondo classes. Every drill, game and challenge is matched to your child's exact stage of brain development — co-created with child psychologists and development experts. It feels like play. But it's building their brain.

Three Age-Specific Tiers

Every class your child attends is specifically designed for where their brain is right now — not where it was last year, and not where it will be next year.

Early Skillz
Ages 3–4
Strength · Confidence · Discipline · Social Skills

At 3–4, children are still developing their motor skills and emotional regulation. They're wobbly, energetic and learning to follow instructions for the first time. Our Early Skillz classes build the foundations — balance, listening, coordination and confidence in a group setting.

Listening & Focus Balance Coordination Group Confidence Following Instructions
"The 3–4 year old brain is in a critical window for motor control and emotional development. Every drill in Early Skillz is designed to maximise this window." — SKILLZ Child Development Experts
Basic Skillz
Ages 5–6
Focus · Confidence · Discipline · Fitness

At 5–6, children are starting school and learning to manage emotions, focus on tasks and break out of shyness. Basic Skillz builds the skills that carry directly into the classroom — self-belief, resilience and the ability to concentrate under pressure.

Self-Belief Resilience School Focus Anti-Bullying Physical Fitness
"It's easy to overestimate 5 and 6-year-olds. Our instructors pay extra close attention to ensure no child slips under the radar." — SKILLZ Child Development Experts
Core Skillz
Ages 7+
Focus · Courage · Resolve · Agility

At 7+, children are bursting with energy and craving challenges — but they grapple with distractions, perfectionism and pushing past fear. Core Skillz builds the grit, concentration and agility that carries into school, sport and life.

Deep Concentration Courage Determination Reaction Speed Anti-Bullying
"They're bursting with energy, craving challenges — but grapple with staying focused and pushing past initial fears." — SKILLZ Child Development Experts
