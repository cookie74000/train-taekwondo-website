The Business Case

Your Team's Health
Is Your Bottom Line

UK sickness absence has reached a 10-year high. According to the CIPD, employees now take an average of 9.4 sick days per year — and the total cost to the UK economy has rocketed to £103 billion annually. For an SME with 50 staff, that's an average of £27,964 in lost productivity every single year.

The good news? Deloitte research shows that for every £1 invested in employee wellbeing, UK businesses see an average return of £5. Physically active workers take 27% fewer sick days, report higher job satisfaction, and perform measurably better under pressure.

Taekwondo is uniquely positioned as a corporate wellness activity: it builds physical fitness, mental resilience, stress management, focus, and team cohesion — all in a single session. And unlike a gym membership, it's something your team will actually turn up for.
